EmptyMyFridge

Team 3

CS 2212 Software Project

For our software project, we are developing EmptyMyFridge, a site where you can put your purchased ingredients to good use. After entering the ingredients you want to use (or all of them for that matter), receive of list of recipes using as many as those ingredients as possible, from across the internet. Better yet, create a user account to save recipes and tailor results based on your favourites.
